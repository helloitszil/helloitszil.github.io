About Me

Hello and welcome!

My name is Zil Sheth. I'm a digital marketer based in Toronto — and this is my little corner of the internet, where marketing meets curiosity, creativity, and just a tiny bit of chaos.

I grew up in Vadodara, Gujarat, where I completed my Bachelor of Business Administration and started building my marketing career early — writing SEO content, running social campaigns, and figuring out how to make brands actually connect with people.

When COVID delayed my Canada move, I didn't wait around. I completed a postgraduate in Marketing Management while working full-time at a startup agency — and was promoted to Marketing Head within 6 months of joining. That detour taught me more than any classroom could.

In 2023, I finally made the move. Since then I've completed two more post-graduate programs: Business Analytics at Seneca College and Content & Digital Marketing at York University.
